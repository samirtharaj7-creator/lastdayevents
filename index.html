What this website is

Last Day Events brings the big picture and the details together. The timeline traces forty major waymarks, the articles slow down for Scripture and Ellen White reflection, and the Three Angels section explains the gospel warning at the heart of the final crisis.

How to use it

Start with the Timeline to see the order of events. Click a waymark to open the study drawer, follow the Learn More link for the full article, then spend time in the Three Angels pages to connect every event to worship, character, and Christ's call to prepare.

How to use

Start broad, then study deeply

Use the site like a study map: begin with the big sequence, then open the details and sources as you need them.

  1. Start with the timeline. Scroll through the major waymarks and notice the “We are here” marker.
  2. Click an event. The side drawer explains what it is, where it fits, what Scripture anchors it, and what Ellen White emphasizes.
  3. Open the full article. Use the “Learn more” link in the drawer to study that event in greater depth.
  4. Study the Three Angels separately. Use the message pages to understand the gospel warning that gives the final crisis its meaning.
