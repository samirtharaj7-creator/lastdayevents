Revelation 14:6-12

The Three Angels' Messages

Overview First Angel Second Angel Third Angel Combined Final Warning
Why these messages matter

The three angels' messages are not merely warnings about future crisis. They are the everlasting gospel applied to the judgment hour, calling the whole world to worship God, leave spiritual confusion, and stand with Christ before probation closes.

Adventist center

In the Adventist understanding, these messages gather together creation, Sabbath, sanctuary, judgment, righteousness by faith, religious liberty, and the final worship test. They prepare the world for the loud cry of Revelation 18.

First Angel Fear God, give Him glory, and worship the Creator Everlasting gospel, judgment hour, Creator worship, and Sabbath restoration. Second Angel Babylon is fallen Religious confusion, rejected light, false doctrine, and God's call out of Babylon. Third Angel Do not worship the beast or receive his mark Final worship test, beast and image, mark and seal, commandments and faith of Jesus. Combined Final Warning The messages unite and swell into the loud cry How Revelation 14 and Revelation 18 combine in the final worldwide appeal.
